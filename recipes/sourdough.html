Sourdough Bread

Ingredients

  • Water: 290 g
  • Sourdough: 80 g
  • Whole wheat Flour: 45 g
  • 000 Flour: 375 g
  • Salt: 8 g

Making the dough

  1. Add the water in a bowl and then add the sourdough until well disolved.
  2. Add the Whole wheat Flour and the 000 Flour and let stand for 1 hour covered with film.
  3. Add the salt and start to knead the dough by hand for a few minutes.
  4. Let it rest for 3/4 hours covered with film.

Making the bread

Add some flour to the table and spread carefully the dough and starts folding towars the center to form a ball, taking care not to lose the air that forms inside.

Then turn over the bread putting the seams down and closing the bread forming a ball.

When the ball is done the dougd is passed into a mold with the seams facing up and is covered with a cloth with some flour.

The dough has to rest for 12/24 hours in the fridge.

Baking the bread

Preheat the oven to the maximum heat around 280/300 degrees celsius.

Put the bread in a Dutch oven and put it in the oven for 20 minutes.

After 20 minutes remove the cover of the Dutch oven and leave it for another 20 minutes in the oven.

Let the bread cool before eating.
