Fried Rice with Quinoa

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup
  • Quinoa: 1/2 cup
  • Oil: 2 tbs (you can use olive or sesame oil)
  • Garlic: 1 tbs
  • Broccoli: 1/2 cup
  • Zucchini: 1/2 cup

Preparation

  1. In a hot pan add the oil and the garlic.
  2. When the garlic starts to go brown add the broccoli in flourettes for 5 minutes.
  3. After 5 minutes add the zucchini and stir all the ingredients in the pan for 2 more minutes.
  4. Then add the rice and the quinoa and keep stiring the ingredients until incorporated.
  5. Keep stiring for a few more minutes and if you want you can add some soy sauce.
  6. Your rice is done. You can chop some cilantro and added to the top when you serve.
